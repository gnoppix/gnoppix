About Gnoppix

History of Gnoppix:

Gnoppix linux live cd was developed in June 2003, and was based upon Debian GNU/Linux 3.0 (woody).

When it was first released, Gnoppix could be compared to Knoppix, with the difference being that Gnoppix used GNOME as the desktop GUI environment. After a successful implementation of the Gnoppix live cd functionality which flowed into Ubuntu in 2004.
Kubuntu was born on December 2004 at the Ubuntu Mataro Conference in Mataró, Spain. Andreas got the approval from Mark Shuttleworth to start the first Ubuntu variant, instead of running Gnone as Desktop using KDE as Desktop, called Kubuntu. Because of some difficulties, disagreements regarding how the free Kubuntu Project was leaded, Andreas stopped his work on Ubuntu/Kubuntu J.Riddell took over.

My Niece requsted during Covid help to install Linux on her Laptop for her remote lessons, I really was shocked, I thought how it could be possible, none of the existing distributions covered after almost 20 years of development the useability of average desktop users, what they are doing? I think this is very poor work after all this time, she wasnt able to do it herself and requested help. I'm older today, hehe, but the world is older too, today we using my Desktops/Laptops in another way how we did before. I'm heavily using a Smartphone for some tasks. And almost many other,like you,do it the same way. But when i saw Kubuntu I thought it is still the same as I left the project. Where are all those KDE Application? Kubuntu today seems like a more Gnome Destop, where is all those love people donated into Kubuntu. Unfortunately, the current Linux Distributions doesn't provide a useable desktop out of the box as we us it today. And here we are again.

With a relaunch of GnoppixNG during Covid-19 in 2021, the decision was made to move to KDE for the GUI, due to its flexibility to run both as a desktop and on mobile devices. In March 2022 my Niece told me, the University celebrated their 5000 installation, Gnoppix is currently their default Desktop. I did not receive (and I do not want) any financial compensation with the university project. I saw it as my duty to support young students with all my experience i've got over the last 30 years. Both Versions are identical.
The current success on the project tells me, I'm on the right direction, therefore I'll share it with everyone else. If you interested to join Gnoppix please contact me on the support email.

Email Services

  • 01 Backup MX

    If you have an outage or a maintanance we will spool all your emails up to 7 days while you work to fix your mail server. Once your mail server is back online we will push the spooled messages received during your outage back to your server automatically. Nothing get lost.

  • 02No Backdoor for Junk

    We utilize greylisting and highly reputable blacklists to curb over 95% of spam. You won't have to worry about a backdoor for spam getting to your mail server by using our backup mx service

  • 03 No Limits

    We don't limit how much email we spool for your domain or how much disk space your emails take up. We'll spool the messages up to 7 days.

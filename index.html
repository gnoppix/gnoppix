About Us

History of Gnoppix: Gnoppix linux live cd was developed in June 2003, and was based upon Debian GNU/Linux 3.0 (woody).
When it was first released, Gnoppix could be compared to Knoppix, with the difference being that Gnoppix used GNOME as the desktop GUI environment.

After a successful implementation of the Gnoppix live cd functionality which flowed into Ubuntu in 2004 we've stopped the Gnoppix Project.

GnoppixNG was during Covid-19 in 2021 relauchned.
